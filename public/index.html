Research Consent

This is a research game designed to study decision-making in social media environments. You will take on the role of a college student navigating their first semester.

By clicking "I Agree" and playing the game, you consent to have your anonymized gameplay choices collected and used for academic research purposes. No personally identifiable information will be stored.

Nexus

Get ready to live your first semester of college on Nexus, the world's most connected social app. The vibe is simple: just be you.

Each week, a new Life Event will pop up. Your feed, your choice—pick the post that feels right. There are no wrong answers, only your story.
